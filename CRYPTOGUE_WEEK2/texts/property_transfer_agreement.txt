PROPERTY TRANSFER AGREEMENT
This Property Transfer Agreement (the "Agreement") is entered into as of January 20, 2025, by and between:
Alice, residing at 123 Maple Street, Springfield, hereinafter referred to as the "Transferor,"
and
Bob, residing at 456 Oak Avenue, Springfield, hereinafter referred to as the "Transferee."
RECITALS
WHEREAS, the Transferor is the sole owner of the property located at 789 Pine Road, Springfield (the "Property"), and
WHEREAS, the Transferor agrees to transfer ownership of the Property to the Transferee under the terms and conditions set forth herein.
NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and agreements contained herein, the parties agree as follows:
1. Transfer of Property
The Transferor agrees to transfer all rights, title, and interest in and to the Property to the Transferee as of the date of this Agreement.
2. Purchase Price
The total purchase price for the Property is $250,000 (Two Hundred Fifty Thousand Dollars), to be paid by the Transferee to the Transferor by wire transfer upon the execution of this Agreement.
3. Representations and Warranties
The Transferor represents that they hold clear and marketable title to the Property, free from all encumbrances.
The Transferee acknowledges that they have inspected the Property and accept it "as is."
4. Closing
The closing of the transaction shall take place on February 1, 2025, at the offices of Springfield Legal Services or another location agreed upon by the parties.
5. Entire Agreement
This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior understandings, whether written or oral.
6. Governing Law
This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of Illinois.
